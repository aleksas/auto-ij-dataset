Description of the procurement:
Sligo County Council is seeking a design team to deliver the plaza as a state of the art, modern civic space with
a linked trail of two lengths. One short trail of 1.5 km within Sligo town and a longer 8.5 km route to Carns Hill.
The successful landscape architect led design team will be responsible for detailed design, detailed cost
estimate, the tender process and construction administration stages of the project. A more detailed description
of the anticipated project stages is included in the sections below.
Sligo County Council is seeking the full range of complimentary consultancy design team services to develop
the project from review of part 8 planning approval, to securing all necessary statutory consents, to complete
tender and construction documentation, tender evaluations, inspection of construction works and issue statutory
certification on completion.
