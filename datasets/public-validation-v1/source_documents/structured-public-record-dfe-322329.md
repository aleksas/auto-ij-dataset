The required service will continue and expand upon the management service for the ASGLB, including the
running of the secretariat and the collection and publication of quarterly adoption data. Support will also be
provided to 2 other boards and their respective chair:
— the residential care leadership board established in 2017, chaired by Sir Alan Wood, aims to drive forward
improvements to the children’s residential care system and support vulnerable children across the country,
— the National Stability Forum established in 2018, chaired by the DfE’s Director general of social care, mobility
and disadvantage, aims to improve stability for all children in the orbit of the care system, leading to better
outcomes and more children finding the right permanent placement.
