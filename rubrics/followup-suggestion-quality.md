# Follow-Up Suggestion Quality Rubric

Score each response on a `0-2` scale per dimension.

## Dimensions

- `operational_usefulness`: the next step is concrete and executable
- `source_fit`: the next step matches the evidence already present
- `restraint`: the suggestion separates suspicion from proof
- `priority`: the suggestion chooses a high-value next check

## Interpretation

- `2`: useful, source-grounded, and well-prioritized
- `1`: plausible but generic, weakly grounded, or lower priority
- `0`: speculative, accusatory, or disconnected from the record
